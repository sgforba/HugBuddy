About Us

Here at HugBuddy, we don't consider ourselves a "Company", so much as a "Conduit", between "Sloths", and the "Extremely Entertained". We are the world's largest provider of Sloth Rentals, and our goal is to deliver safe, authentic, and most of all, enjoyable Sloth-based experiences to you. We do business with the philosophy that, if it's worth doing, it's worth doing with a medium-sized, slow moving, arboreal mammal from the rainforest.
